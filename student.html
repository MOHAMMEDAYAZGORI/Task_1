Ayaz School


India's south-eastern coastline region is home to the state of Andhra Pradesh. It is the tenth-most populous state and the seventh-largest state in terms of area. The largest city in the current Andhra state is Amaravati, with Amaravati serving as its capital. According to the 2011 Indian census, Andhra Pradesh has an overall literacy rate of 67.41%. Government, nonprofit, and private schools provide primary and secondary education.


The state's School Education Department oversees and regulates all of these institutions. Telugu and English are used as the primary teaching languages, with a very small number of students choosing Urdu, Hindi, Kannada, Odia, and Tamil.
